## OPTION 1: Publish on Replit Itself (EASIEST - 1 Click!)

**Do this RIGHT NOW:**

1. On Replit, click the green **Run** button at the top
2. Wait for your website to load on the right side
3. Look at the top right corner where you see your website
4. Click the **Share** button
5. Click **Publish**
6. Click **Publish Project**
7. Done! Your website now has a permanent URL!

**Your website will be live at a URL like:**
```
https://hemlata-jr-associates-website.replit.dev
```

Anyone in the world can visit this URL! üéâ

## OPTION 2: Use Vercel (Also Very Easy)

Since you already linked GitHub, this is super simple:

1. Go to: https://vercel.com
2. Click **Sign in** ‚Üí **Continue with GitHub**
3. It will show your project
4. Click your project name
5. Click **Import Project**
6. Click the blue **Deploy** button
7. Wait 3 minutes
8. Your website is live!

**Your website will be live at a URL like:**
```
https://hemlata-jr-associates-website.vercel.app
```

---

## OPTION 3: Use Netlify (Also Very Easy)

1. Go to: https://netlify.com
2. Click **Sign up** ‚Üí **GitHub**
3. Click **Authorize Netlify**
4. It will show your repositories
5. Click your project
6. Click **Deploy**
7. Wait 3 minutes
8. Your website is live!

**Your website will be live at a URL like:**
```
https://hemlata-jr-associates-website.netlify.app
```
